Signature Dishes

Pho Tan My prides itself with cooking and serving only the highest quality ingreidents. We source our meat and vegetables locally in order to ensure freshness.

Pho With Beef Broth

Vietnamese beef noodle pho is an easy soup to fall in love with. Those chewy noodles, that savory broth, the tender slices of beef — all those crunchy, spicy, herby garnishes we get to toss on top. On a cold evening, after a rough day at work, when we’re sick, on a lazy weekend afternoon — a bowl of piping hot pho is pretty much always a good idea.

Grilled Pork With Broken Rice




This is love in a bowl! If you’ve had bún thịt nướng you know what I’m talking about. You have your sweet bits, sour bits, caramelization, some crunch, and aromatic herbs in a single, colorful arrangement. This was one of our more popular dishes.
